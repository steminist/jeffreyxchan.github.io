Mission Statement

UCLA ACM-W is one of the committees amongst the larger ACM organization on campus. ACM-W is a community of women intent on pursuing a career or interest in computing and technology. We host programs and offer resources to engage more women and advance their contributions to technology.

Our Impact

Our mentorshop program pairs freshman and sophomore students with upperclassmen majoring in computer science! UCLA is an intimidating place, and this program helps the younger girls become more comfortable and successful!

Officer Team

The officer team includes nine ambitious, passionate computer science majors. They are in charge of organizing events, contacting corporate sponsors, mentoring lowerclassmen and running organizational logistics! If you're interested in applying for a position, contact Enyu!

Join Us

Want to join ACM-W and begin supporting fellow female computer scientists? Our next meeting will be held in Spring Quarter! In the meantime, come out to one of our many professional and social events. Come hang out with us in ACM's new clubhouse in Boelter 2673!
